How to Play

Goal

Reveal every safe cell without hitting a mine.

Controls

Tap / Click a cell to reveal it

Long-press / Right-click to flag a suspected mine

Tap a number to auto-reveal neighbors (chord)

Numbers

Each number tells you how many of its 8 neighbors are mines. Use logic to deduce which cells are safe!

Power-Ups

🔍 Reveal Safe — reveals a random safe cell

🛡️ Shield — blocks one mine hit

🎯 Scan — shows mine count in a row or column

🧲 Magnet — relocates mines from a 3×3 area to the board edges

🔬 X-Ray — reveals mines in a 5×5 area

❤️ Lifeline — auto-saves you from one mine

Win games to earn power-ups!

Modes

Challenge — 100 levels with modifiers & power-ups

Quick Play — race for speed ratings (💎🥇🥈🥉)

Daily — same board for everyone, online leaderboard

Keyboard

R restart • 1-5 power-ups • Esc close

What's New

v1.5 — April 21, 2026

  • Smarter Greg-par — Daily par is now a model fit to real completion times, not just move count. Easy boards get a lower par, tough boards get more.
  • Per-feature breakdown — After each daily, see exactly what drove par: +14s medium moves · +6s mystery · +13s baseline.
  • Handicaps — Your personal par adjusts for your typical pace across recent dailies, golf-style. Faster players see a lower target, so "beating par" means the same thing for everyone.
  • History timeline — Open the leaderboard to see a chart of your last 30 dailies against par. Green is under, red is over.

v1.4 — March 29, 2026

  • 120 levels — Challenge mode extended with 20 new Grandmaster levels (L101–120).
  • No more 50/50s — Every board is verified solvable after modifiers are applied. No guessing required.
  • Pressure plates redesigned — Reveal all safe neighbors to disarm. Timer now scales dynamically with puzzle difficulty.
  • Mirror pairs — Two adjacent cells swap their displayed numbers, highlighted with matching borders. Up to 3 pairs per board at higher levels.
  • Wormhole visuals — Paired cells share matching background colors. Combined counts above 8 have distinct colors.
  • Theme polish — Number colors brightened across all dark themes. Particle effects boosted. New flag emojis for better visibility.
