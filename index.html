How to Play

Basics

Left-click to reveal a cell

Right-click to place/remove a flag

Click a number to chord-reveal (if flags match)

Reveal all safe cells to win!

Power-Ups

🔍 Reveal Safe — reveals a random safe cell

🛡️ Shield — survive one mine click

🎯 Scan — shows mine count in a row/column

⏸️ Freeze — pauses timer for 15 seconds

🔬 X-Ray — reveals mines in a 5×5 area

🔓 Decode — reveals all hidden ? cells (Fog)

Earn power-ups by winning games!

Game Modes

Challenge — progressive difficulty with power-ups

Timed — race against the clock

Fog of War — limited vision, hidden numbers, creeping fog

Daily — same board for everyone today

Keyboard Shortcuts

R — restart game

1–7 — use power-ups

Esc — close modal
