How to Play

Basics

Left-click to reveal a cell

Right-click (or long-press) to place/remove a flag

Click a number to chord-reveal (if flags match)

Reveal all safe cells to win!

Power-Ups (Challenge & Fog)

🔍 Reveal Safe (1) — reveals a random safe cell

🛡️ Shield (2) — blocks one mine, shows defused bomb

🎯 Scan (3) — shows mine count in a row/column

⏸️ Freeze (4) — pauses timer for 15 seconds

🔬 X-Ray (5) — reveals mines in a 5×5 area

🔓 Decode (6) — reveals all hidden ? cells (Fog)

Earn power-ups by winning games!

Game Modes

Challenge — 50 levels of increasing difficulty with power-ups

Timed — pick a difficulty, race for your best time. Speed ratings: 💎 Diamond, 🥇 Gold, 🥈 Silver, 🥉 Bronze

Fog of War — limited vision, hidden numbers, creeping fog

Daily — same board for everyone today. Online leaderboard!

Keyboard Shortcuts

R — restart game

1 Reveal • 2 Shield • 3 Scan • 4 Freeze • 5 X-Ray • 6 Decode

Esc — close modal
